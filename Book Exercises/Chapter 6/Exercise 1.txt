Title: Person

exercise: Use a dictionary to store information about a person you know.
Store their first name, last name, age, and the city in which they live. You
should have keys such as first_name, last_name, age, and city. Print each
piece of information stored in your dictionary.

Answer:

dict_person = {'first_name': 'John', 'last_name': 'Doe', 'age': 35, 'city': 'London'}

print(dict_person['first_name'])
print(dict_person['last_name'])
print(dict_person['age'])
print(dict_person['city'])
